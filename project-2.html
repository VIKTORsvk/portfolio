Hive Dive

An endless-running on-rail shooter where you pilot a remote-controlled drone into hordes of enemies. Shoot down the invaders, fly through colorful Patagonia, and get the highest score!

Contribution / Responsibilities

I began my work on Hive Dive as a Gameplay Programmer, prototyping various flight and control systems. I also developed a complex, modular tech tree system, which unfortunately had to be cut due to scope limitations.

In the latter half of development, I transitioned into the role of Producer and Vision Holder, stepping in when the project was at risk. I discovered a strong ability to motivate and lead, successfully rallying the team to refocus and deliver a complete game. I was responsible for planning, sprint management, retrospectives, build reviews, final game design decisions, and overseeing the Steam release. I also contributed to polishing gameplay by handling bug fixes, tuning enemy spawns, and setting encounter pacing.

On the design side, I created the weapon overheating mechanic, including side bar indicators and crosshair feedback, as well as the dash bar UI element. I also helped with sound design throughout development. The team’s positive feedback on my leadership and design contributions was a pivotal moment that inspired me to shift my career focus from pure programming to game production and design.
