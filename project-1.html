Iron Line

Iron Line is a tower defense game where you are one of a few, train conductors capable of traversing on the Iron Line. Will you survive the onslaught, or get an express ticket to the afterlife?

Contribution / Responsibilities

I was the Programming Lead on Iron Line, responsible for sprint planning, task distribution, and ensuring smooth integration of game systems through agile processes. I designed and implemented key progression systems, including train station flow, contract selection, and wave spawning. I also contributed to game design, initiating and leading turret upgrade system discussions, and implemented two upgrade tiers for the minigun.

In addition, I served as the project’s Sound Designer, creating and implementing all in-game sound effects. I tailored each sound for impact and immersion, fine-tuning attenuation and concurrency settings in-engine to ensure the best audio experience for players.
