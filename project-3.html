Magnetic Mayhem

Hive Dive is a local multiplayer physics brawler built in Godot 4 for up to 4 players. Players use magnetic powers to grab and fling hazards like grenades, missiles, and saws in chaotic arena battles.

Contribution / Responsibilities

I designed and developed the entire prototype, including custom controller-based player joining, magnetic interaction systems, modular hazard spawners, and round-based level progression. I also handled visual polish, responsive game feel, and balanced arena design through extensive playtesting.
